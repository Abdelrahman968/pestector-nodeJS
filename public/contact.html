Frequently Asked Questions

How do I identify a plant disease?

Upload an image of your plant using the Pestector app, and our AI will analyze it to identify potential diseases or pests.

Can I track my plant history?

Yes, go to the "History" page to view all your past scans and their results.

What if I don’t get a response?

If our AI can’t identify an issue, you can contact support below, and we’ll assist you manually.
