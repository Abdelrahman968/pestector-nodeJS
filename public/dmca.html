Pestector DMCA Policy

Pestector is in compliance with 17 U.S.C. 512 and the Digital Millennium Copyright Act ("DMCA"). It is our policy to respond to any infringement notices and take appropriate actions under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act ("DMCA") and other applicable intellectual property laws.

If your copyrighted material has been posted on Pestector or if links to your copyrighted material are returned through our website and you want this material removed, you must provide the following information:

  • Provide evidence of the authorized person to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.
  • Provide sufficient contact information so that we may contact you. You must also include a valid email address.
  • Give the full address (URL) that allows direct access to relevant content.

If the requested information is incomplete or false, we will ignore your request.

Send notice of violation by email to: dmca@pestector.com

Processing your request may take up to three business days.
