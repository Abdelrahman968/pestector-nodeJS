Pestector Official Rules!

Please review these rules to ensure a respectful and safe community. Failure to comply may result in penalties.

Pestector Official Rules

Forum Rules

Failure to comply with the following rules may result in warning points and/or a ban:

  • Do not create or use multiple accounts.
  • English is the only permitted language on our forum.
  • Do not attempt to evade a ban. If you want to appeal a ban, use the contact form.
  • Do not engage in hacking, infecting, phishing, or scamming other members.
  • Do not post fake giveaways.
  • Posting referral links without clearly indicating that they are referral links is prohibited.
  • Do not impersonate other members.
  • Do not participate in black-hat activities such as hacking, fraud, or leaking personal information.
  • Do not sell maliciously or illegally obtained goods.
  • Do not intentionally steal, harm, or misuse any members of the forum.
  • Do not advertise other forum sites.
  • Do not bully or harass members.
  • Do not post or sell fake programs or services.
  • Do not sell fake, public, or leaked methods or exploits.
  • Do not misuse or abuse the report system.
  • Do not engage in low-quality content, such as spamming, sales trashing, mass tagging, cross-posting, asking for loans, racism, adult or shocking content, character evading, misleading thread titles, etc.
  • Do not sell any tools/programs unless you own the source and/or resale rights.

User Agreement

By signing up on Pestector, you agree to the following terms:

  • Adhere to our Terms of Service.
  • Adhere to our Privacy Policy.
  • Pestector is not responsible for any on-site or off-site deals.
  • Creating multiple accounts will result in the permanent closure of all accounts.

Hide System

Members can use the hide system in the following ways:

  • By likes: members can choose to hide their content until it reaches a certain number of likes, up to a maximum of 1000 likes.
  • By days spent on the forum: members can choose to hide their content until they have been active on the forum for a certain number of days, up to a maximum of 365 days.
  • By groups: members can choose to hide their content and make it visible only to certain groups on the forum.
  • By clicking "like" to see content: members can choose to hide their content and require other members to click "like" in order to reveal it.
  • For special users: members can choose to hide their content and make it visible only to certain users who have been granted special access.

It is strictly forbidden to set values higher than the maximum limits stated above. If any member is found to have set higher values, their message will be edited to the minimum values or deleted altogether.

Please use the hide system responsibly and with consideration for other members. Do not use it to intentionally hide important information or to be malicious towards others.

Any misuse of the hide system may result in disciplinary action, including warnings, suspension, or a permanent ban from the forum.
